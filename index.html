Site History

The products that once passed through these doors have been used by an extraordinary number of people worldwide, providing the building itself with a history rich in fabrication. The premises, that have now been transformed into a sleek and modern trade centre, were originally owned and operated by Avon Inflatables.

This was the location at which Avon designed and developed some of their best-in-class RIBs (Rigid Inflatable Boats), which were adopted for use by the military, the coast guard and many other essential organisations. This allowed Avon Inflatables to redefine the boating industry from their modest Llanelli based factory and provide a plethora of skilled jobs to local tradesmen.

On Arrival

When we first arrived on the site it was clear that this was going to be a large project. Set in approximately 2.7 acres of land, with a footprint of around 35,000 square feet, the derelict factory seemed as though it would never end.

We began working on site almost immediately after the documents had been signed and the progress that our team made within the first few weeks was astonishing. In around two weeks, the exterior had been totally transformed.

From a severely overgrown wilderness a clear and organised working environment began to emerge. After years of neglect, the building was finally receiving the attention it so desperately needed.

The Design

From the earliest design sketches on scrap pieces of paper to the 3D rendered CAD drawings produced by our architecture team, the design of Dafen Trade Park has been the primary focus since day one. As a company we take pride in creating functional and beautiful spaces that deliver on even the most acute aspects of usability.

We notice the finer details that others may not and aim to provide a solution that is not just satisfactory, but one that has been fine tuned to ensure the highest level of quality.

Completion

At the end of our journey, it's clear that our commitment and passion to the project has been worth it. The pleasure we have taken from watching the development take shape is almost too much to put into words and it has made us extremely proud of what our skilled team of craftsmen have been able to achieve.

Supporting the local community is an extremely important goal and one that resonates throughout the history and development of Dafen Trade Park. This will remain the case throughout the running of the park and we hope to see that many local businesses embrace the space that we have to offer.
