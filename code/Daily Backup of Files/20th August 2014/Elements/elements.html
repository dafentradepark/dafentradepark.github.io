Article title:

When arriving on site we were struck.

Article text:

This site had been available to purchase for several years yet it had been overlooked by many potential investors and developers over that period. Being a Llanelli based company we had been aware that the site was for sale however, like a large number of passers by we had not really achknowledged the old Avon Inflatables site. It was overgrown and had been derelict for years.

However in early 2014 Robert Rees, director and CEO of the Rees Group, organised to view the site. Upon arrival it was clear that this was not something that could be overlooked any longer. The old factory was the perfect project.
